Office AI Agent Lab

Office AI Agent Lab is a research team focused on developing intelligent office automation systems. We leverage large language models and multi-agent collaboration techniques to automate office workflows, enhance work efficiency, and redefine human-AI collaboration paradigms.

Our research directions include LLM-based agents, multi-agent collaboration, procedural memory architecture, agent-user interaction, and agent skills.

Openings: We are looking for highly motivated students to join us. Please email us at your.email@example.com if interested.
